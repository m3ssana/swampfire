JUAN BREAKS ALL RECORDS: Player Now Has 8 Achievements to Aspire To

By Reporter Rita Stormborn | Swampfire Correspondent

In a stunning development that has left game developers scrambling to implement even MORE motivational systems, Juan's achievement system has reached unprecedented levels of milestone-mania. The bespectacled protagonist can now unlock a staggering eight distinct achievements, ranging from "First Loot" to the coveted "Globe Trotter" designation.

"We wanted players to feel constantly rewarded," said an anonymous developer, who was last seen muttering about localStorage and localStorage about persistent state tracking.
